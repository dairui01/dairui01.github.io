Rui Dai

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Vision (CV) and Artificial Intelligence (AI) at the Inria under the supervision of Prof. Francois Bremond. I also have a close collabration with Toyota Motor Europe (TME).

In 2018, I received my Master and Engineer degree in Electronics from Toulouse University. In 2016, I obtained my Bachelor degree in Computer Science from Beihang University (BUAA) in Beijing, China.
