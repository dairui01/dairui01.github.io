Rui Dai obtained his Bachelor degree in Computer Science from Beihang University (BUAA) in Beijing, China. In 2018, he received the Master degree in electronics and signal processing from ENSEEIHT, University of Toulouse. Since 2018, He has been a Ph.D. student in Computer Vision (CV) and Artificial Intelligence (AI) at the Inria under the supervision of Prof. Francois Bremond.

My research interests include human action understanding, scene understanding and transfer learning.
